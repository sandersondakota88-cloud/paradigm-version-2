CT ENGINE / RUNTIME
Phase 3 splits the host into two engines coordinating through the shared field. The CT engine processes operations sequentially, owns persistent state, and tracks execution-scope delta. The ER engine resolves constraints in parallel and tracks render-scope delta. Per SE-06, neither commands the other; both read the field and produce delta at their own positions. The gap between exec and render delta is observable from the host but neither engine consults the other's delta to decide what to do.
