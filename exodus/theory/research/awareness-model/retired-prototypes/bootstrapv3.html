v3 introduces sub-cascades. When a family of constraints consistently reduces delta when consulted together (fidelity above threshold), it promotes itself into a named sub-cascade with its own local delta at its scope (SE-01 reflexive). Addressing a sub-cascade by name in an input produces a moderate naming bias - the field resolves faster when called by name, and the slow layer accumulates a preference for that addressing pattern over time.
